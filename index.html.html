Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Welcome to SpeakWell. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and Phone Number

2. Microphone & Speech Data

We use your microphone for real-time speech training. Audio may be processed temporarily to provide feedback but is not stored permanently.

3. AI Chatbot

SpeakWell includes an AI chatbot to assist users. Conversations may be processed to improve responses and enhance user experience.

4. Session Scheduling

When you schedule sessions, we store timing and preferences to help you manage your training routine effectively.

5. Notifications

We may send notifications for reminders, updates, and training alerts. You can control notification preferences in settings.

6. Training Tips & Recommendations

Your usage data helps us provide personalized training tips and suggestions to improve your speaking skills.
